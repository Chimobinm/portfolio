Notary Public Services

Need reliable and professional notarization services? We provide fast, secure, and accurate notarization for a wide range of documents — affidavits, contracts, power of attorney, and more. Serving areas around Gainesville and Ocala, Florida.

Request Notarization Appointment

Services Provided

Document Notarization

  • Acknowledgment of signatures on deeds, affidavits, powers of attorney
  • Certification of true copies of original documents
  • Notarization of contracts, agreements, and commercial documents
  • Witnessing and certifying execution of legal instruments

Administration of Oaths & Affirmations

  • Swearing or affirming affidavits and statutory declarations
  • Oaths for court documents, immigration forms, and official statements
  • Commissions for taking affidavits and declarations

Protests & Other Notarial Acts

  • Protesting bills of exchange, promissory notes (when applicable)
  • Certificates of due execution and other notarial certificates
  • Verification of identity for international documents

Mobile & Remote Notarization

  • Home, office, hospital, or location visits (where permitted)
  • Online/remote notarization services (if legally available in your jurisdiction)
  • Fast turnaround for urgent documents

Important Information

Requirements

  • Valid government-issued photo ID (passport, national ID, driver's license)
  • Original documents to be notarized (copies usually not accepted for most acts)
  • All signatories must be present (unless remote notarization is used)
  • Fees are charged per notarial act / signature (standard rates apply)

Availability & Turnaround

Same-day service often available for urgent matters. Appointments preferred; walk-ins accepted subject to availability. Weekend/public holiday service possible with prior arrangement.

Professional Standards

All services performed in strict compliance with notarial laws and ethical guidelines. Full confidentiality maintained. Commissioned and insured Notary Public.
